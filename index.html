WHY CHOOSE US

Choosing 3 Rivers Guide Service for your next fishing adventure means partnering with seasoned experts in river navigation and angling. We know the waters, and that ensures both a productive and an enjoyable fishing experience. Our variety of boats are set up and chosen for each journey based on the specific river or lake we're tackling, so they're tailored to enhance your fishing experience and provide you with the best access to prime fishing spots.

Our lead guide, Wes, brings a lifetime of fishing knowledge and expertise to each expedition. His angling skills and familiarity with the waters make him not just a guide, but a valuable fishing companion. With Wes at the helm, you'll benefit from insider tips on the best fishing techniques and locations, giving you the best chance at a rewarding catch and an enjoyable experience. Fishing with 3 Rivers Guide Service is more than just casting a line; it's about immersing yourself in America's beautiful nature, guided by experts with a passion and commitment to the sport. Join us to experience the thrill of the catch and the peace of the great outdoors!

PRICING

Packages:

  • Half-Day: $500
  • Full-Day: $700

Deposit and Cancellation:

  • 1/2 trip cost will be due at booking. The second half is charged on the day of the trip before hitting the water.
  • Deposits will not be refunded within 14 days of the trip.
  • Before 14 days, deposits can be refunded with a $25 cancellation fee.

Includes:

  • Up to 3 people (Bigger groups accomodated, contact us for accurate pricing).
  • Water, drinks, snacks.
  • Lunch (Full-Day only, add to Half-Day for $20/person).
  • Rods, reels, and tackle. Feel free to still bring your own!

Not Included:

  • Fishing license. Purchase prior to meeting your guide!
